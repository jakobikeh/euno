Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2, 2024

Introduction

Euno ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our task and calendar management app.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: Used for account authentication and identification
  • User ID: Generated to link your account to your data

User-Generated Content

  • Tasks: Task titles, descriptions, due dates, and completion status
  • Labels: Custom labels and categories you create
  • Calendar Entries: Events and appointments you add

Technical Data

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (anonymized)
  • Crash Data: Technical error reports (anonymized)
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics (anonymized)

How We Use Your Information

App Functionality

  • Authenticate your account and provide secure access
  • Store and retrieve your tasks and calendar data
  • Enable core app features and personalization
  • Implement security measures and prevent fraud

Product Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app features
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Optimize app performance
  • Plan new features based on user needs

Customer Support

  • Provide technical support and assistance
  • Resolve account-related issues
  • Address user feedback and concerns

Third-Party Services

We use Supabase as our backend service provider for:

  • User authentication and account management
  • Data storage and synchronization
  • Server infrastructure and security

Supabase processes your data according to their privacy policy and our instructions.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and monitoring

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can delete your account at any time, which will remove your personal data from our systems.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of certain data collection

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
